# Known issues & technical debt

> Long-lived bugs, workarounds, stubs, and design debt. Short-lived items
> belong in `handover.md`. Each entry says what is wrong, why it is OK to
> live with for now, and the trigger that will cause us to fix it.

## Priority guide

- **P0 (Block)** — User-facing bugs. Fix before any new feature work.
- **P1 (High)** — Development infrastructure. Fix to prevent future regressions.
- **P2 (Medium)** — Correctness gaps. Fix during related phase work.
- **P3 (Low)** — Design debt. Address in dedicated cleanup work or specific phase.
