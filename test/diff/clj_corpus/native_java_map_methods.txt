;; java.util.Map + Map.Entry read methods on NATIVE collections (D-379, F-013).
;; clj's collections implement java.util.Map/Map.Entry, so perf libs call these as
;; interop on native colls (flatland.ordered's OrderedMap: `(.get ^Map backing m k)`
;; / `(.getKey e)` / `(.val e)`). cljw routes `.member` on a native coll to the
;; clojure.core equivalent (clojure_lang_method.zig). clj-oracle-verified 2026-06-10.
(.get {:a 1 :b 2} :a)
;;=> 1
(.get {:a 1} :z)
;;=> nil
(.getKey (first {:a 1}))
;;=> :a
(.getValue (first {:a 1}))
;;=> 1
(.val (first {:a 1}))
;;=> 1
(.key (first {:a 1}))
;;=> :a
(.size {:a 1 :b 2})
;;=> 2
(vec (.values {:a 1 :b 2}))
;;=> [1 2]
(vec (.keySet {:a 1 :b 2}))
;;=> [:a :b]
